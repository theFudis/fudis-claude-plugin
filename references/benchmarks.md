# Industry Benchmarks — Restaurant Retention & Growth

**Source:** Bloom Intelligence 2025 · Black Box Intelligence · Restaurant365
**Region:** Primarily North America. Use as directional reference; calibrate to local market when possible.

## Retention benchmarks
| Metric | Industry average | Top performers | Region |
|---|---|---|---|
| Annual guest churn | 78.8% | ~35–45% | North America |
| Annual retention rate | 21.2% | 55–65% | North America |
| 1st-visit return rate (90 days) | 25% | 35–45% | North America |
| Guest frequency | 1.21x/month | 1.5x/month | Global |
| Revenue at risk per location/year | ~$375K USD | — | North America |

> **Non-US markets:** apply the percentage benchmarks (churn %, retention %) — they are broadly stable across markets. Convert the revenue figure using the restaurant's own average ticket × guest base, not the USD number.

## Frequency impact
Moving from 1.21x → 1.5x guest frequency = **+22% revenue** with zero new customer acquisition cost.
This is the universal lever — frequency math works the same in any currency.

## Framing guidance
- Lead with the visceral number: "78.8% churn = [operator's estimated annual revenue at risk]" — calculate from their data, not the USD figure
- Personalized > broadcast: always lead with quality over reach
- Round percentages to 1 decimal
- Use the currency from the restaurant's own data for all monetary figures
