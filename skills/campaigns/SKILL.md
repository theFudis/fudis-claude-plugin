---
description: Generate campaign ideas or identify customers to target for promotions, reactivation, or VIP outreach. Use when the operator asks about marketing, campaigns, who to contact, or how to bring back lost customers.
---

Use $ARGUMENTS to accept an optional focus: "winback", "vip", "new", or leave empty for a full campaign brief.

Steps:
1. `campaign_triggers` — customers flagged for action today (at-risk, win-back candidates, birthday, milestone)
2. `customer_segments` — segment size overview for targeting context
3. `customer_clv` — top 10 by predicted lifetime value (for VIP campaigns)

Based on the data, suggest 2-3 concrete campaign ideas with:
- Target segment (who to contact)
- Message angle (why reach out now)
- Suggested channel (WhatsApp message, personal call, table reservation offer)
- Estimated impact (customers affected, revenue potential in the restaurant's currency)

Be specific — naming specific guests and actions beats generic campaign advice.
