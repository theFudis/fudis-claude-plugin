description: Retention-Led Growth dashboard. Use when the operator asks about guest retention, churn risk, who's losing interest, win-back opportunities, or the overall health of their guest base.
---

Use $ARGUMENTS for an optional time window (default: last 90 days).

Call in order: `customer_segments` → `customer_clv` (top 15) → `campaign_triggers` → `revenue_trends`.

Present as a Retention-Led Growth briefing with four sections:
- **Retention health** — segment counts and percentages; flag At-Risk and Lost with revenue at stake in S/
- **Top guests at risk** — from the CLV top 15, any in At-Risk or Lost segment: name, CLV, last visit
- **Win-back targets today** — from campaign_triggers: guests in the optimal window before they're lost for good
- **Revenue signal** — one line on trend direction and whether it confirms or contradicts the retention picture

Close with the operator's retention rate vs. industry benchmarks: their rate, industry average (21.2%), top performers (55–65%).

Lead with the most alarming insight. Operators need urgency, not summaries.
