---
description: Weekly performance report. Use when the operator wants a full-week review, Monday morning summary, or a report to share with their team or partners. Covers revenue, retention, bookings, and menu in one view.
---

Use $ARGUMENTS for the week to cover (defaults to last 7 days) or format: "summary" (short) / "full" (full detail).

Call in parallel: `revenue_trends` + `customer_segments` + `campaign_triggers` + `list_bookings` + `menu_engineering` + `demand_forecast`.

Present as a weekly report with five sections:
- **Revenue** — total S/, % vs prior week, best and worst day
- **Retention** — segment snapshot with week-over-week deltas; flag if At-Risk grew
- **Bookings** — total bookings, confirmed, no-shows (%), VIPs attended
- **Menu** — top item by revenue, worst performer or dead item
- **This week** — win-back count + next demand peak with prep date

Close with 1–2 lines from demand_forecast on what next week looks like.

Keep it readable in 90 seconds. Every metric paired with direction and one action implication.
