---
description: Revenue trends and demand forecasting. Use when the operator asks how sales are trending, what days are strongest, or wants to plan staffing and prep for upcoming demand.
---

Use $ARGUMENTS to focus: "trends" (trends only), "forecast" (forecast only), or leave empty for both.

Call `revenue_trends` + `demand_forecast` in parallel.

Present two sections:
- **Trend** — direction (growing/flat/declining with % vs prior period), best and worst day, weekly pattern if visible
- **Next 30 days** — next demand peak and trough with dates and forecast in S/; top 3 high-demand dates with a concrete operational action each (staffing, inventory, reservations)

Lead with the trend direction — operators want to know if revenue is up or down before anything else.
